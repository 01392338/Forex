Currency Converter

Enter a FROM currency and a TO currency in the three digit format (e.g. USD for US dollars or EUR for Euros) along with an amount of the FROM currency (don't enter any currency symbols).

For example, if you want to know how many Euros $100 US dollars converts to, you'd enter USD in the FROM box, EUR in the TO box, and 100 in the amount box.
